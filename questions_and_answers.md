###### 1. Write the `correct answer` from the following options and give an explanation (2-5 lines).

```javascript
let greeting;
greetign = {};
console.log(greetign);
```

- A: `{}`
- B: `ReferenceError: greetign is not defined`
- C: `undefined`

<details><summary><b>Answer</b></summary>
<p>

#### Answer: A

<i>Here firstly greeting was declared but it was not initialized. The 2nd variable is a global variable with value of {}. However it is not declared with let, var or const thats why it behaves like a global variable</i>

</p>
</details>
